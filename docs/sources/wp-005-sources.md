# WP-005 Sources — Compliance kickoff

Research sources for the `docs/compliance/` v0 pack. All accessed **2026-07-05**.

## Primary (fetched and verified)

- https://nita.gov.gh/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Data-Protection-Act-2012-Act-843.pdf — accessed 2026-07-05 — Full text of Ghana's Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843), NITA copy; used to verify §§20, 24–33, 35, 37, 45–59, 83, 96. Archived: `act-843-data-protection-act-2012.pdf`.
- https://dataprotection.org.gh/ — accessed 2026-07-05 — DPC homepage; confirms controllers **and** processors must register; links registration portal and fee schedule.
- https://dataprotection.org.gh/registration/ — accessed 2026-07-05 — DPC registration page; required particulars, portal link, renewal window (3 months before expiry to 7 days after), registration manuals.
- https://app.dataprotection.org.gh/ (register: /auth/register, login/renewal: /auth/login) — accessed 2026-07-05 — Current DPC registration/compliance portal (RegSys). Note: not `register.dataprotection.org.gh`.
- https://dataprotection.org.gh/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/DPC-NEW-FEES.pdf — accessed 2026-07-05 — Current DPC fee schedule (extract of L.I. 2512, Fees and Charges (Miscellaneous Provisions) (Amendment) Regulations, 2025): Specialised 5,000 / Large 2,340 / Medium 1,170 / Small 156 GHS, etc. Archived: `dpc-fees-li-2512-2025.pdf`.
- https://dataprotection.org.gh/report-a-breach/ — accessed 2026-07-05 — DPC breach-reporting channel and contact details; no fixed reporting deadline stated.
